To Configure iSCSI Targets:

  • Add Target: Use this button to create a new iSCSI target, which can have its own LUNs and configuration.
  • Add LUN: Click this button to add a new Logical Unit (LUN) under the selected Target ID. Each LUN represents a storage volume available over iSCSI.
  • Save: After making changes, click "Save" to apply and store the iSCSI target configurations. Note: If a target has no LUNs when saved, it will be automatically deleted.
  • Reload: Click this to refresh the page and discard any unsaved changes.
  • Field Description: Click on a field label for a short descirption.

Ensure to configure the Interfaces, Inbound/Outbound Accounts, and LUN paths before saving. Targets without configured LUNs will automatically be deleted.
